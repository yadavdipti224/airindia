About US

‘AISATS’, a leading gateway services provider in India, is a 50-50 joint venture between Air India Limited and SATS Limited, a key player in gateway services and food solutions in Asia. ‘AISATS’ is defined by its people – they are our greatest strength. Their commitment to seamless delivery and customer delight is what enables us to fulfill our brand promise of service excellence. This has been the hallmark of our business for over 12 years. With deep domain knowledge of the aviation ecosystem, we offer passenger experience services, ramp handling, load control, flight operations, baggage handling, and aircraft services.

Board of Directors

MR NIPUN AGGARWAL

Director

Mr. Nipun Aggarwal is an Engineer from Delhi College of Engineering and MBA from IIM Kolkata with over 25 years of experience in Investment management, Strategy, M&A, Finance & Operations across a wide range of industries. He is currently a Senior Vice President at Tata Sons Private Limited and looks at Strategy and M&A for the Tata Group. Over the past five years, as part of the Group Chairman’s Office, he has been instrumental in shaping the strategy and transformation of a number of Tata Group companies. He has driven several important initiatives for the Tata Group including the Bhushan Steel Acquisition, consolidation of the Group’s Defence businesses and the launch of Tata Electronics. Most recently, he led the Air India acquisition for the Group. He is a Director on the Board of several Tata Group Companies such as Tata Projects, Tata Housing, Tata Electronics, Tata Urban Infra and Tata Power Odisha Discoms. Prior to joining the Tata Group, Nipun worked as Executive Director with Standard Chartered Bank in the Corporate Banking department responsible for coverage of strategic clients. And prior to that, he was a Director with DSP Merrill Lynch in the Investment Banking and M&A department where he led the coverage for clients across Infrastructure, Industrials and Metals & Mining sectors, and also executed several marquee capital markets and M&A transactions. Prior to DSP Merrill Lynch, he worked with BP Plc. in India and UK as Commercial Advisor for large upstream Oil & Gas projects. He started his career with Larsen & Toubro Limited as Graduate Engineer Trainee.

Mr. Vinod Hejmadi

Director

Mr Vinod Hejmadi is a Commerce Graduate, a Chartered Accountant and a Rank Holder in Final CA. Mr Vinod Hejmadi, Director – Finance is the Board Member of Air India from November, 2015. He has 28 years of experience in various departments of Airline Industry and has handled important portfolios like Revenue and Expenditure accounting, Audit, Insurance, Fuel Hedging, Fuel & Oil, Aircraft leasing and Budget. He was also a key member of the team set up for formulating and finalizing the Turnaround Plan of Air India and was also a key member in the committee for post-merger activities. Mr Vinod Hejmadi has also held international positions such as Finance Manager in Switzerland & North Africa based out of Geneva and Chicago. Mr Hejmadi was also part of several tender committees and lease committees for lease of Narrow Body aircraft, negotiations with lessors, finalization of lease agreements. He is also on the Board of subsidiary companies of Air India – Air India Air Transport Services Ltd, Alliance Air Services Ltd., Air India Engineering Services Ltd, Air India Charters Ltd. and Air India SATS Airport Services Private Limited

MS MEENAKSHI MALLIK

Director

Ms Meenakshi Mallik took over the charge of Director (Commercial), Air India Limited on 17th June 2019. She has more than 28 years of experience in various areas of Commercial functions of the airline. She is an undergraduate in Science and holds a post graduate diploma in Management (MBA) and has worked in various capacities in Air India ranging from Revenue Management & Pricing, Market Planning & Scheduling, Sales & Marketing to Commercial Administration. Earlier she was serving as Executive Director (Commercial and Revenue Management) where she contributed in bringing about a paradigm shift in the running of the section transforming it into a strategic unit of the company. She has aggressively pushed for expansion of Air India network by opening of new stations, more bilateral arrangement with other carriers enlarging Air India’s reach through its interline partners. She has been responsible for the formulation and implementation of various schemes geared towards increasing revenue and reduction of overall cost including that of distribution in Air India.

MR KERRY MOK

Director

Mr Kerry Mok is the President and CEO of SATS and joined the company in June 2018. Prior to this, he was the CEO of YCH Group since August 2017. Mr Mok is a seasoned executive with more than two decades of experience in supply chain management and logistics, he has held various senior appointments prior to his move to YCH Group, including his role in Goodpack Limited as the acting Chief Executive Officer and Chief Operating Officer. Before that, he held the position of Managing Director, Strategy - Operations and was also head of the ASEAN Supply Chain Strategy practice for Accenture. Mr Mok was also previously Senior Vice President - Global Head of Technology Sector and APAC Technology Sector & Service Logistics with DHL, accountable for the strategy and growth of the Global Technology Sector for DHL Supply Chain. He has been an active contributor to tripartite initiatives. Mr Mok has been a member of the Future Economy Council's Connectivity Sub-Committee since 7 January 2022 and serves on the Ministry of Communication and Information’s Infocomm Media Master Plan 2025 working group. Mr Mok graduated from Monash University, Melbourne, Australia with a Bachelor of Business, Accounting (First Class Honours).

MR KOK KHONG SEAH

Director

Mr Seah joined SATS as Chief Financial Officer in September 2017. He oversees the group's finance operations, strategic investment management and monitoring, group treasury, insurance and investor relations. Mr Seah has over 25 years of investment banking, direct investments and financial management experience. He has held senior leadership roles in corporate finance and investment management, and has conducted corporate advisory and mergers and acquisitions activities in Asia. Before joining SATS, Mr Seah was the Chief Financial Officer of SMRT Corporation Ltd, where he was primarily responsible for driving changes to the business and financing structure of the Group. At SMRT, Mr Seah led a special task force that developed and facilitated the transition of SMRT Trains Limited to the new rail financing framework, and managed the subsequent privatisation of SMRT by Temasek Holdings in 2016. Mr Seah sits on various Boards of SATS' subsidiaries and associate companies. Mr Seah graduated with Bachelor of Science (First Class Honours) in Mathematics from Queen Mary College, University of London and obtained his Masters degree in Business Administration from the London Business School. He qualified as a Chartered Accountant in London, UK, and has been conferred an Advanced Diploma in Corporate Finance (CF) and Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales (FCA).

MR RAMANATHAN RAJAMANI

Director

Mr. Ramana has more than 15 years of experience in both the Military and Civil Aviation sectors. Mr. Ramana started as an engineer in the Defence Science and Technology Agency ensuring the reliability of fighter jets for the Republic of Singapore Air Force (RSAF) in 2001. He then moved to Indeco Engineers in 2006 to lead several turnkey projects for both commercial and military ground operations. He subsequently joined SATS in 2011 as the head of the Engineering Team which developed and won the first patent for the organisation. He has since held several positions in SATS Gateway Division and also headed a fully owned subsidiary, receiving Singapore’s prestigious National Productivity Award in 2016. Mr. Ramana graduated from the National University of Singapore with a Bachelor of Engineering as well as a Master of Science, majoring in Logistics. Mr. Ramana was subsequently awarded an Executive Master of Business Administration from the Nanyang Technological University and the International Air Transport Association (IATA) under the joint sponsorship of SATS & Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore (CAAS).

Mr. Bobban Jose

Chief Operations Officer

Mr. Bobban Jose is currently the Chief Operations Officer at AISATS. As Head of Operations, Bobban is responsible for managing the comprehensive Ground & Cargo Handling Operations of 5 AISATS stations (Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mangalore & Trivandrum).
