AISATS has not authorized any person or organization to either collect money or arrive at any monetary arrangement in return for a job at AISATS. We would like to bring to your notice that AISATS follows a meritocratic recruitment process. We do use professional recruitment firms as needed to search for suitable candidates where the offers are always made directly by AISATS and not by third parties. Please verify the authenticity and genuineness of any job offer. Please note that we will not in any way be responsible or liable for any loss that may be caused to anyone on account of their dealings with such fraudulent intermediaries and any fraudulent communication made by them shall not be treated as any kind of offer or representation by AISATS. In case, you come across any person or organization demanding money in lieu of an offer letter for a job opportunity in AISATS, you are encouraged to bring such matters to our attention by writing to us at feedback.hr@aisats.in.
