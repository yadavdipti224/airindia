Our Services

With capabilities achieved through years of servicing world-class clients and a deep understanding of the aviation eco-system we offer passenger experience, ramp handling, baggage handling, flight operations and Cabin dressing services.

Let's explore some of our best services-

Passenger Services

As a customer service organization, we understand the importance of offering unique service experiences to our airline' passengers, each day. Our full range of passenger services include check-in & departures, boarding assistance, special assistance for passengers with reduced mobility & unaccompanied minors and meet & greet service.

Apron Services

Our reputation as a leading ground handling agency is a legacy that was built over years of trust and credibility. Getting a flight ready for departure requires seamless co-ordination, accurate weight balance, perfect load distribution and quick turnarounds. Our efficient ground experience team are trained in all aspects of ramp handling and operations of the airlines they serve. This ensures the demand of the airline are always met with the highest level of service, even during irregular operations. Our ramp services are ISAGO compliant, which simply means that it is a safe and secure working environment for aircrafts, customers and passengers while also eliminating any potential risk of operating delays.

Cargo Handling Services

Bengaluru, in Southern India, houses AISATS exclusive cargo warehouses – the Air Freight Terminal and COOLPORT. Our Terminals are built on the philosophy that every cargo box shipped has a unique story to share. From flowers to pharmaceuticals, every pallet moved enables us to connect lives and deliver smiles. At the forefront of our reliable and efficient operation is our insatiable urge for adopting technology. That's why we are pioneers in the industry. Our aim is to stay ahead of the curve. When you partner with us you are serviced by a large team of cargo experts, that deliver on end to end cargo ground management supported by technologically advanced tools.

Value Added Services

We offer disinfection of Aircraft Cabin using UV-C (Ultra Violet C Band) Technology based Robotic Device. The usage of this UV-C Device (code named: UV - VECTOR Air) disinfect the surfaces from germs, bacteria and virus It takes only 10 minutes to disinfect the surfaces thoroughly.

AISATS also offers cabin baggage checking services using smart solution developed by iboarding. Cabin Baggage checker is an electronic gauge that employs artificial intelligence to analyze weight and size in relation to the airline's cabin baggage policies.
